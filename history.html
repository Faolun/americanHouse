Built by the Moravians over 230 years ago. The American House is a significant landmark within a village of landmarks in Hope, New Jersey. A testament to the stonemasons craft, the structure has endured the decades since its founding fathers made their mark in the northwest section of the state.


Now, with the restorers love of detail, the building has been restored to create a unique business location within this most historic setting. Hand-hewn beams and stone walls define eight quaint and comfortable office suites in four floors of space. Painstaking preservation of fireplaces, wood treatments, original doors, window casements, and the use of original flooring in the hallways and lower level spaces combine to bring the past most charmingly into the present.
